Delta Laser Writer
C02 or UV Laser Marking for Tablets & Capsules
The Delta C02 or UV Laser writer can laser write an alphanumeric, logo or
trademark on a tablet, soft gelatin capsule, or caplet containing titanium
dioxide (TiO2). The fully adjustable hopper allows the product to flow smoothly
to the laser station and can laser mark 50,000 to 70,000 pieces per hour.
