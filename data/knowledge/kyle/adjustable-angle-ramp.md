Adjustable Angle Ramp Printer
Rotogravure Printing System for Tablets & Capsules
High-output precision printing system uses edible inks to place logos onto
pharmaceutical and confectionary products. Features an adjustable Ramp
Feed Angle to optimize feed based on product flow characteristics. Bulk
product is fed into the hopper, where each product is efficiently singulated and
gravity-fed into carrier bar pockets for precise positioning. The continuous
motion, multiplelane carrier bar conveyor feed system exposes the top surface
for inspection and printing. Requires compressed air, power & vacuum for
operation.
