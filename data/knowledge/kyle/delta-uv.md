Delta UV Laser
UV Laser Marking for Tablets & Capsules
The Delta UV Laser writer can laser write an alphanumeric, logo or trademark
on a tablet, soft gelatin capsule, or caplet containing titanium dioxide (TiO2).
The fully adjustable hopper allows the product to flow smoothly to the laser
station and can laser mark 50,000 to 70,000 pieces per hour.
