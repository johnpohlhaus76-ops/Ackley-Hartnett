Delta Printer
Rotogravure Printing System for Tablets & Capsules
The Delta Ink Printing machine is a versatile and user-friendly printing ma-
chine. This machine can print on many different shapes or sizes of tablets, soft
gelatin capsules, and two-piece hard gelatin capsules. The Delta Ink Printing
Machine can print one or both sides of a product, as well as axile or spin print.
