IBM Ink Printer
Flatbed Printing System for Tablets & Capsules
The IBM Ink Printing Machine is our high-speed, large scale production printing
machine. With outputs over 300,000 pieces per hour, the IBM prints all tablets,
soft gelatin capsules, and caplets with ease. This flatbed machine comes with
a bulk hopper and flood feeder for maximum fill rates and can print one side of
a tablet.
