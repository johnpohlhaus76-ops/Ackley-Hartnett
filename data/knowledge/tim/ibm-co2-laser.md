The IBM CO2 Laser Writer is our high-speed, large output laser writing
machine. With outputs over 300,000 pieces per hour, the IBM marks all soft
gelatin capsules and caplets with ease. This flatbed machine comes with a
bulk hopper and flood feeder for maximum fill rates and can print one side of a
tablet.
